The vehicle

A drawing that learned to fly.

Before it was thrust and telemetry, it was ink — a line of longing pulled taut against gravity. We keep the blueprint here as a reminder that every machine leaving the ground was first an act of imagination, drafted by hand, annotated in the margins, and believed in long before it burned.
